Hello and welcome players!


Over my years as a game master, I have learned that guiding players in making characters who are already established with each other, and who are group focused, makes for a better campaign overall. The following content isn't meant to stifle you as players or meant to belittle anyone. The entire purpose is for players to make characters that will be productive, useful, and long lasting in the adventure we will be embarking upon. Below is a list of the pages and their content to help you in this task.

  • Home: This just so happens to be where you are right now!

  • Adventure Setting: This gives you the scoop on the adventure. It is a PDF designed by yours truly to give you an intro, character creation guidance, and some information on the location and locals.

  • Character Creation: This page helps guide you in creating your character. It gives you guidelines on the most useful stats, most useless stats, damage types to avoid, appropriate alignments, and so forth.

  • Backstory Creation: Here you will find guidance with writing your backstory. Stuff such as who you should know, what NPC you should tie yourself to, how you should act towards the group, and etc...

  • Quick Checklist: This page combines the important points from the Character Creation and Backstory Creation pages to give you an all encompassing list in one place.

That's all folks! Make sure to reference this site everytime you create a character as it will help you build a character appropriate for both the group and adventure. If you ever have questions about rules or anything else you can feel free to ask me, or also check out Arhives of Nethys . Which have every rule, option, and feature of PF2e listed for free with a search bar to find it quickly and easily.
