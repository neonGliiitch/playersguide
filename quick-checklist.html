Character Creation

  • Ancestry: No problematic ancestries like skeleton. Talk to me about more rare ancestries.

  • Heritage: Talk to me about more rare heritages.

  • Background: Acolyte, bounty hunter, farmhand, field medic, guard, hunter, martial disciple, miner, nomad, and scout are the best backgrounds. There are adventure exclusive ones in the PDF I provide. Talk to me about more rare backgrounds.

  • Class: Cleric is the best class for this adventure, though many others excel. Classes built around downtime, diplomacy, and deception wonâ€™t be useless but will pale when compared to others.

  • Attributes: Follow the rules in the Core Handbook on this.

    • Skills: Religion, occultism, society, thievery, and athletics are the most useful core skills. Cavern Lore, Otari Lore, and Spirit Lore are the most useful lore skills.

  • Feats: Canny Acumen, Incredible Initiative, and Toughness are the most useful feats. Talk to me about rare feats.

    • Languages: Akloa, Elven, Draconic, and Undercommon are the most useful languages.

  • Alignment & Deities: No evil, nothing that you will use as an excuse to be evil.

  • Gear: Follow the rules in the Core Handbook on this. Anything uncommon or above consult with me.

  • Damage: Avoid poison as a primary damage type. Positive damage will see lots of use. Have backups in case monsters are immune to your type of damage.

Backstory Creation

  • Group Oriented & Team Work : Make a character who works with the group and is group friendly.

  • Background & Level Appropiate: Your character's background should be part of their back story and appropriate for your level.

  • You're the Hero: Your character wants to save the day and help people.

  • Location Connection: You should have a connection to the location and the group before the campaign starts.

  • Character Growth: Create a character who will grow and not stay stagnant.

  • NPC Connections: Tie your character to the important NPC of the adventure. Try to find others to connect with as well.

  • In Depth: Put some depth into your character so you can connect with them better and I can use their backstory for session building.

This section is meant to be straight to the point without all the fluff and text of the other pages. I do encourage you to read the other pages if you're creating your first character. But this short list should help you when creating any future characters.
