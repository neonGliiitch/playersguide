Character Creation

  • Ancestry Heritage Background Feats : Anything common is fine. Uncommon or above consult with me.

  • Gear: Follow the rules in the Core Handbook on this. Anything uncommon or above consult with me.

  • Attributes: Follow the rules int he Core Handbook on this.

  • Alignment & Deities: No evil, nothing that you will use as an excuse to be evil.

  • Damage Skills Languages: Consult the Character Creation page for detailed instructions on damage use in dungeon

Backstory Creation

  • Group Oriented & Team Work : Make character who works with group and is group friendly.

  • Background & Level Appropiate: Your character's background should be part of their back story and appropiate for your level.

  • You're the Hero: Your character wants to save the day and help people.

  • Character Growth: Create a character who will grow and not stay stagnant.

  • NPC Connections: Tie your character to the important NPC of the adventure. Try to find others to connect with as well.

  • In Depth: Put some depth into your character so you can connect with them better and I can use their backstory for session building.

This section is meant to be straight to the point without all the fluff and text of the other pages. I do encourage you to read the other pages if you're creating your first character. But this short list should help you stay when creating any future characters.
