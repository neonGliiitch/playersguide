Welcome to character creation!


When you are creating characters there are some very important aspects to keep in mind. Below I will go through a list of guidelines for you to follow as you create your characters. Remember, the goal here isn't to ruin your fun. It's to make sure all players come to the table with proper characters ready to tackle the adventure ahead.


The follow guidelines are for Abomination Vaults. This will change when we switch between Adventure Paths. So remember to check what adventure this is for. Remember characters are made at session 0. Feel free to show up with some ideas and rough outlines, but making our characters together ensures good group harmony and is just a lot of fun.

  • Ancestry: There is no limitation to ancestries. However, if you wish to play one above common rarity please consult me first.

  • Heritage: The same guidelines apply here. No restrictions but consult me if you want to use something other than common.

  • Backgrounds: Now we are talking the background you will choose during character creation. Not the one you write later on afterwards. This also follows the same guidelines of just consult me if you want to use a background above common.

  • Class: There are no regulations of classes. However, Abomination Vaults is meant to be a dungeon drawl. Classes who excel at diplomacy or deception will not find a lot of use. Classes that also depend on lots of downtime between adventures will also struggle to play.

  • Attributes: We will be using the system as outlined within the Core Rulebook. There is the optional flaws. Those are for you to choose if you want to use, they are not a variant rule.

  • Alignment: No evil characters. This means no evil alignments at all. It also excludes using Chaotic Neutral as an excuse to be evil.

  • Deities: Call them a god or religion, do not worship evil deities that task their followers with evil deeds.

  • Feats: Going back to the guidelines of ancestry and heritages. Try to stick with common feats. If wishing to go with something above common consult me first please.

  • Equiptment: The expectation of gear in PF2e is that you start with common gear. If you wish to start with gear above common please consult me on the issue. You can find the following guidelines and rules for making higher level characters on Archive of Nethys here.

  • Damage Types: In Abomination Vaults there is a lot of resitences to poison damage that it is highly suggested to avoid this as a type of damage. While on the other hand positive damage will be very useful in the upcoming adventure. Also there are creatures immune to all pyshical damage (this is different than only pyshical damage from non magical weapons) and all magical damage. They are rare enough that you don't need to build a character around these concepts, but having some type of backup damage would be useful.

Arlight folks! The above guidlines are what you should follow when making a character. If you have any questions please get in touch with me.
